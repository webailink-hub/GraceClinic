Comprehensive Care

Medical services designed for your health and peace of mind.

We provide a wide range of medical services to treat illness, prevent disease, and keep our community healthy. No appointment necessary for urgent needs.

Urgent Care & Illness

  • Cold, flu & COVID-19 evaluation
  • Fever & sore throat (Strep testing)
  • Ear, sinus & respiratory infections
  • Bronchitis & mild pneumonia
  • Allergies & asthma exacerbations

Physicals & Screenings

  • School & sports physicals
  • DOT & Pre-employment physicals
  • Workers' compensation care
  • TB clearance & health screenings
  • On-site rapid testing & lab services

Wellness & Treatments

  • IV hydration & vitamin therapy
  • Vaccinations (Flu, COVID, Tdap)
  • Nebulizer treatments
  • Basic dermatologic procedures
  • STD treatment & counseling

Cash-pay procedures & injections available. We strive for accessible care for all.

Nuestra Promesa / Our Promise

Dedicated to clinical excellence and compassionate care.

At Alma Hispana Medical Clinic, we believe that everyone deserves high-quality, accessible healthcare. Our visual identity—the heart and the medical cross—represents our core mission: combining the universal symbol of care with clinical excellence.

We understand the unique needs of the Lynwood community. Whether you need a walk-in evaluation for a sudden illness, a pre-employment physical, or specialized treatments like IV hydration, our bilingual staff is ready to provide reassuring, patient-first care.

Compassionate

Empathetic, understanding, and always prioritizing your comfort from the moment you walk in.

Accessible

Clear communication, no complex medical jargon, and open 7 days a week to serve you.
